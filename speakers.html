Dave Del Rosario

An FAA staff Sir Dave guided and instructed us on the school's software and hardware uses, which teaches students how to operate airplanes through simulation without actual flight. They detailed the software and hardware features, such as providing a realistic simulation of flying conditions to prepare students effectively. As well as explaining about how to enroll and study in their school.

Jerome Saddi

Sir Jerome is an engineer who taught us about locating incoming and outgoing ships, measuring their distance, and ensuring they stay within designated boundaries. This tracking is crucial for the safety and security of ships, as well as for predicting their arrival times, which aids in developing predictive patterns for future planning purposes. He has also shared about the different areas within Subic Bay and how they were formed and how big and how deep they are compared from one to another.
